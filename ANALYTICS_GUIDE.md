## üö® RED FLAGS TO WATCH

### Warning Signs:

**1. Bounce Rate > 70%**
- Problem: People leaving immediately
- Solution: Improve homepage, faster loading

**2. Avg Duration < 30 seconds**
- Problem: Not engaging
- Solution: Better content, clearer CTA

**3. Zero Organic Traffic**
- Problem: Not indexed or ranked
- Solution: Check Google Search Console

**4. High Exit Rate on Contact Page**
- Problem: CTA not clear or broken
- Solution: Test WhatsApp button, add phone number

**5. No Mobile Traffic**
- Problem: Mobile site issue
- Solution: Test responsive design
